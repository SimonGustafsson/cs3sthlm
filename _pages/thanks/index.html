The CS3STHLM 2018 Summit has come to an end and the team is working with all post-production activities, one of them is to say THANK YOU to all people involved in the event!

The CS3STHLM 2019 Summit will, as always, happen in week 43 and dates will be October 21-24 - please mark your calendar! Already there are thoughts and plans on how to improve and expand the Summit even more, news about that will be communicated in later newsletters. If you have ideas or opinions, do share them with us!

Best wishes from the CS3STHLM team!
