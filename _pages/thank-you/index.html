Thank You!

The CS3STHLM summit of 2017 was yet another success, and we would like to express our warmest gratitude to all of you, because it would not have been possible without YOU!

The team behind CS3STHLM just came back after a much needed week of vacation and we now have to do some follow-up work to wrap this years conference up. We have got feedback from some of you attendees, the majority of them very positive, but also some points to analyze and try to make better.

 As we start to plan for next years conference, we would like to hear from you. Please let us know your experiences from your visit to Stockholm and the CS3STHLM summit – what can we do better?

If you would like to keep in touch and to know what we are planning for the future, please press the blue button on our home page cs3sthlm.se to receive our newsletters.

Once again – THANK YOU!

Erik, Robert and the rest of the CS3STHLM Crew
