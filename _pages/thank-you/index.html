Thank You!

The CS3STHLM summit of 2017 was yet another success, and we would like to express our warmest gratitude to all of you, speakers and trainers, because it would not have been possible without YOU!

The team behind CS3STHLM just came back after a much needed week of vacation and we now have to do some follow-up work to wrap this year's conference up. We have got feedback from attendees, the majority of them very positive, but also some points to analyse and try to make better. The most important feedback for us is that ALL of the responders have said that they would recommend CS3STHLM to a friend or a colleague and that they would consider returning another year!

As we start to plan for next years conference, we would like to hear from you. Please let us know your experiences from your visit to Stockholm and the CS3STHLM summit – what can we do better?

Once again – THANK YOU!

Erik, Robert and the rest of the CS3STHLM Crew
