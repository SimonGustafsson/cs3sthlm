Practical Details

Nalen, Stockholm

The venue of our choice, Nalen, has a long and fascinating history. Built in 1888 it has over the years seen a wide range of events ranging from athletic competitions to religious meetings. Today Nalen hosts over a hundred concerts and conferences annually in its carefully renovated premises.

Closest Airports

41,5 km

Arlanda is Sweden's biggest airport and acts like a central hub for the region of Stockholm and Scandinavia.

Closest Subway

Hötorgets T-bana

Exit Olof Palmes gata, turn right on to Olof Palmes gata and take the stairs up to Malmskillnadsgatan. Continue straight along David Bagares gata. Take the first right onto Regeringsgatan, Nalen is on the left side.

Östermalmstorgs T-bana

Exit towards Birger Jarlsgatan, turn right and follow Birger Jarlsgatan past Stureplan to Engelbrektsplan. Turn left onto David Bagares gata, up the stairs to Regeringsgatan. Turn left around the corner and you are at Nalen.
